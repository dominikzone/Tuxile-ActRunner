# Path of Exile 1 — Complete Campaign Guide (Acts 1–10)

---

## 📖 Legend

| Symbol | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| 🔵 | **Waypoint** — always grab these! |
| ⚔️ | **Boss** |
| ❗ | **Quest** |
| 📦 | **Quest Item / Key NPC** |
| 🏆 | **Trial of Ascendancy** |
| 💡 | **Tip** |
| ⚠️ | **Warning — don't skip this!** |

---

---

# ACT 1

---

## Zone 1 — The Twilight Strand

Pick up weapons and gems as you move up the beach — they're lying in plain sight. At the top of the coast you'll meet your first boss.

⚔️ **HILLOCK**
> 💡 Use attack + move to dodge his swings. Spam all potions freely — town is right after.

---

## Zone 2 — Lioneye's Watch *(Act 1 Town)*

Your hub for the entire act. Three vendors worth knowing:

- **Nessa** — potions, jewelry, caster gear
- **Tarkleigh** — weapons, armour + your **first skill gem** *(grab it before leaving!)*
- **Bestel** — gambling and respec points

⚠️ **Grab your first skill gem from Tarkleigh before heading out.**
Exit north to **The Coast**.

---

## Zone 3 — The Coast

Don't linger — XP is poor. Do three things and move on:

1. Grab the 🔵 **Waypoint**
2. **Skip Tidal Island** for now — you'll return later via waypoint
3. Head straight to **The Mud Flats**

---

## Zone 4 — The Mud Flats

❗ **Quest: Breaking Some Eggs**

Skip Fetid Pool entirely. Your objectives:
1. Slay Rhoas and collect **3 Eggs**
2. Place eggs in the wall at the far end to open **The Submerged Passage**

> 💡 Rhoas charge in a straight line — strafe sideways to dodge the stun.

🎁 *Quest Reward: Level 4 Gems*

---

## Zone 5 — The Submerged Passage *(first visit)*

1. Grab the 🔵 **Waypoint**
2. Return to town to shop for Level 4 gems and gear
3. Use the waypoint to go back to **The Coast**, then visit **Tidal Island**

---

## Zone 6 — Tidal Island

❗ **Quest: Mercy Mission**

Fight your way to the bottom of the zone.

⚔️ **HAILRAKE**
> 💡 He casts frost/chill effects — keep moving, use attack + strafe.

🎁 *Quest Reward: Quicksilver Flask + Gem*

After defeating Hailrake, exit to character selection to return to town instantly. Collect rewards, then waypoint back to **The Submerged Passage**.

---

## Zone 5 — The Submerged Passage *(second visit)*

Follow the paths until you find either:
- A hole in the ground → **The Flooded Depths**
- A zone exit → **The Ledge**

> 💡 **Recommended strategy:** Leave a Town Portal at the Flooded Depths entrance, then proceed to The Ledge and Lower Prison. Return to Depths later via the portal to avoid backtracking.

---

## Zone 7 — The Flooded Depths

Search the zone for the boss.

⚔️ **THE DWELLER OF THE DEEP**

🎁 *Quest Reward: Passive Skill Point*

Proceed back through The Submerged Passage to **The Ledge**.

---

## Zone 8 — The Ledge

Walkthrough zone — follow the linear path and grab the 🔵 **Waypoint** in the middle.

> 💡 Just after the waypoint, three stones arranged in a triangle point toward the exit.

---

## Zone 9 — The Climb

Another walkthrough zone. 🔵 **Waypoint** is near the start. At the far end, a wall door leads to **The Lower Prison**.

---

## Zone 10 — The Lower Prison

Reaching this zone completes the first part of **The Caged Brute** quest. Return to town to buy Level 8 gems before continuing.

🏆 **Trial of Ascendancy** — complete it while you're here!

🎁 *Quest Reward: Level 8 Gems*

Proceed to **The Upper Prison**.

---

## Zone 11 — The Upper Prison

🔒 **Hidden Flask Stash:**
1. Look for a Strongbox icon that appears unreachable behind a wall
2. Find the **SWITCH** on a nearby pillar
3. Click it to open the wall and claim random flasks

Find the entrance to **The Warden's Quarters**.

---

## Zone 12 — The Warden's Quarters

Follow the long path to its end, backtrack on a parallel path, then go through the small rooms at the top.

⚔️ **BRUTUS**
> 💡 Focus on sustain — use potions aggressively. Unlike PoE2, Brutus does **not** fully respawn on death — you can continue the fight across multiple deaths.

🎁 *Quest Reward: Level 10 Gems*

Proceed to **The Prisoner's Gate**.

---

## Zone 13 — The Prisoner's Gate

🔵 **Waypoint** is at the zone start. A fake exit at the end of the main path is blocked by Piety.

> 💡 When the path is blocked, search the opposite area near the starting point. Hug the wall and follow the path that leads "deeper" — this feels dense at first, but persevere. You're looking for **The Ship Graveyard**.

---

## Zone 14 — The Ship Graveyard *(first visit)*

Grab the 🔵 **Waypoint**, then find **The Ship Graveyard Cave** — a cave entrance near a sunken/broken ship somewhere in the center of the zone.

---

## Zone 15 — The Ship Graveyard Cave

Head directly away from the zone entrance until you reach the boss.

⚔️ **STRANGLECHARM**
After defeating her, grab the 📦 **Allflame** quest item. Exit through the wall behind her — it takes you back to **The Ship Graveyard**.

---

## Zone 14 — The Ship Graveyard *(second visit)*

You emerge right in front of Fairgraves.

⚔️ **FAIRGRAVES**

❗ **Quest: The Marooned Mariner**

🎁 *Quest Reward: Passive Skill Point (in town)*

After defeating him, find the entrance to **The Cavern of Wrath** — it's a wall entrance somewhere in the zone.

---

## Zone 16 — The Cavern of Wrath

🔵 **Waypoint** at the start. Walkthrough zone — follow it to **The Cavern of Anger**.

---

## Zone 17 — The Cavern of Anger

Walkthrough zone — follow it to the wall entrance of **Merveil's Lair**.

---

## Zone 18 — Merveil's Lair

⚔️ **MERVEIL** *(Act 1 Final Boss)*

> 💡 She deals heavy cold damage.
> - Avoid the large cyclones that stretch skyward
> - Kill any minions she spawns immediately
> - She has multiple phases — stay mobile

After defeating Merveil, exit north to begin **Act 2** via **The Southern Forest**.

---
---

# ACT 2

---

## Zone 1 — The Southern Forest

Head straight north through this walkthrough zone to reach the Act 2 town.

---

## Zone 2 — The Forest Encampment *(Act 2 Town)*

Vendors:
- **Greust** — weapons and armour
- **Yeena** — gems, potions, jewelry + free item identification
- **Silk** — gambling and respec points

⚠️ Other NPCs here will give you key quest items as you progress — talk to them after completing quests.

Exit north to **The Old Fields**.

---

## Zone 3 — The Old Fields

Walkthrough zone — follow the path. The Den is a side area you can safely skip.

---

## Zone 4 — The Crossroads

🔵 **Waypoint** in the center of the zone — **bookmark this area**, you'll return multiple times.

From the Waypoint, three paths branch out. Take them in this order:
1. **NORTHWEST** → The Chamber of Sins
2. **NORTHEAST** → The Broken Bridge
3. **SOUTHEAST** → The Fellshrine Ruins

Head **Northwest** first.

---

## Zone 5 — The Chamber of Sins (Level 1)

Walkthrough zone — 🔵 **Waypoint** is roughly in the center. Find the staircase to Level 2.

---

## Zone 6 — The Chamber of Sins (Level 2)

Two objectives here:

1. 🏆 **Trial of Ascendancy** — complete this first (usually on the opposite side from the boss)
2. ⚔️ **FIDELITAS** — find him at the end of a long narrow path

After killing Fidelitas:
- Pick up the 📦 **Baleful Gem**
- Speak to Helena
- Return to town

🎁 *Quest Reward: Level 16 Gems*

Return to **The Crossroads** and head **Northeast** to **The Broken Bridge**.

---

## Zone 8 — The Broken Bridge

❗ **Quest: Deal with the Bandits**

Follow the main path to the broken bridge. You'll choose to **Help** or **Kill** each of the three bandits.

> 💡 **Recommendation:** Kill all three bandits to gain **Passive Skill Points**. Some builds benefit from helping certain bandits — check your build guide. Either way, you can change this decision later.

⚠️ **After killing Kraityn, pick up his Amulet!** You need all 3 bandit amulets.

Return to town, then head back to **The Crossroads** and go **Southeast** to **The Fellshrine Ruins**.

---

## Zone 9 — The Fellshrine Ruins

Large and full of distractions — **ignore them all and stay on the path.**

The path ends at a graveyard-like archway area. Angle slightly north to find the entrance to **The Crypt**.

---

## Zone 10 — The Crypt (Level 1)

🔵 **Waypoint** at the start.
🏆 **Trial of Ascendancy** — complete it here.
Then head to Level 2.

---

## Zone 11 — The Crypt (Level 2)

Navigate to the far end of this winding dungeon. Find the yellow ❗ and boss.

⚔️ **ARCHBISHOP GEOFRI**

Behind him is an Altar — grab the 📦 **Golden Hand**.

🎁 *Quest Reward: Passive Skill Point (turn in at town)*

After turning in the quest, return to town and head to the **bottom entrance** of The Forest Encampment to reach **The Riverways**.

---

## Zone 12 — The Riverways

Acts as the southern crossroads.

1. Follow the main path to the 🔵 **Waypoint** in the middle
2. Note the side path to **The Wetlands** — you'll return here
3. For now, continue along the main path to **The Western Forest**

---

## Zone 13 — The Western Forest

Follow the main path to the 🔵 **Waypoint**, then all the way to the end.

⚔️ **CAPTAIN ATERI**

❗ After defeating him, take the 📦 **Thaumetic Emblem** and socket it into the wall behind his camp.

🎁 *Quest Reward: Passive Skill Point (turn in at Lioneye's Watch in Act 1!)*

**Don't return to town yet.** Look for a torch off the main path — follow it to find **Alira's Camp**.

⚔️ **ALIRA** — Kill her (or help her if your build guide recommends it).

⚠️ **Pick up Alira's Amulet!**

Search the opposite wall of the zone for the entrance to **The Weaver's Chambers**.

---

## Zone 14 — The Weaver's Chambers

Walkthrough zone — find the entrance to **The Weaver's Nest**.

---

## Zone 15 — The Weaver's Nest

⚔️ **THE WEAVER**

After defeating her, grab 📦 **Maligaro's Spike** and return to town.

🎁 *Quest Reward: Level 18 Gems*

Return to **The Riverways**, then head north from the Waypoint to **The Wetlands**.

---

## Zone 16 — The Wetlands

Travel north to find **Oak's Camp** and the 🔵 **Waypoint**.

⚔️ **OAK** — Kill him (or help if your build requires it).

⚠️ **Pick up Oak's Amulet!**

Near the Waypoint, search the wall for the entrance to **The Vaal Ruins**.

---

## Zone 17 — The Vaal Ruins

Large, labyrinthine zone. Search until you find the 📦 **Ancient Seal** — a large purple orb. Click it and proceed to **The Northern Forest**.

---

## Zone 18 — The Northern Forest

🔵 **Waypoint** near the entrance. Two connecting zones:

1. Go south → **The Dread Thicket** — only required if you haven't unlocked your Hideout yet (look for the Fleur-de-Lis icon)
2. Then head north → **The Caverns**

---

## Zone 19 — The Dread Thicket *(optional — Hideout unlock)*

Only visit this if you haven't unlocked your Hideout. Clear the bosses Aidan and Nadia, then return to The Northern Forest and head north to The Caverns.

---

## Zone 20 — The Caverns

Long, alleyway-filled dungeon. Follow the path around the bottom to the 🔵 **Waypoint**.

⚠️ A locked **Door** here requires the 📦 **Apex** quest item — obtained by turning in all 3 bandit amulets in town. If you're missing amulets, go back now.

Head north through the Caverns to the staircase leading to **The Ancient Pyramid**.

---

## Zone 21 — The Ancient Pyramid

Multiple walkthrough levels — just go up the stairs at each floor.

⚔️ **VAAL OVERSOUL** *(Act 2 Final Boss)*

> 💡 Click the altar in the center and wait — it takes a moment to spawn. The fight has multiple phases but none are particularly punishing. Turn on the music — the OST is genuinely excellent.

Exit north through the wall to begin **Act 3** via **The City of Sarn**.

---
---

# ACT 3

---

## Zone 1 — The City of Sarn

⚠️ **This is not a simple walk-through zone.**

You **must**:
1. Defeat **The Guard Captain**
2. Talk to the **Clarissa** NPC

Skipping Clarissa here will brick your Act 3 progression. After completing this, head all the way north to **The Sarn Encampment**.

---

## Zone 2 — The Sarn Encampment *(Act 3 Town)*

> 💡 You can walk around the encampment OR exit to character selection and rejoin to teleport to the center instantly.

Vendors:
- **Hargan** — weapons and armour
- **Clarissa** — gems, potions, jewelry + free item ID
- **Maramoa** — gambling and respec points

⚠️ **The Sarn Arena (north side of town) — DO NOT GO HERE.** This is PvP only, it has no campaign value.

📦 There's a zone with a green Skull icon on the minimap — this is the **Aspirant's Plaza** (Labyrinth). You'll return here after completing all 6 Trials.

Exit northeast to **The Slums**.

---

## Zone 3 — The Slums

Walkthrough zone. Note the locked gate to **The Sewers** — you'll return after Zone 4. Grab the 🔵 **Waypoint** if you see it. Head to **The Crematorium**.

---

## Zone 4 — The Crematorium

Two objectives:
1. 🏆 **Trial of Ascendancy** — complete it first
2. ⚔️ **PIETY** — defeat her for the **Lost in Love** quest

After killing Piety, pick up 📦 **Tolman's Bracelet**, return to town, and talk to Clarissa to get the **Sewer Keys**.

Head back to **The Slums** and use the keys to open the locked gate to **The Sewers**.

---

## Zone 5 — The Sewers

Multiple objectives — you'll return here later:

1. ❗ Find and open **3 Stash areas** to collect the **Bust** quest items *(Victario's Secrets)*
   - 🎁 *Quest Reward: Passive Skill Point*
2. Grab the 🔵 **Waypoint** near the Undying Blockage — note its location for later
3. **Skip Sister Cassia** — league content, not required
4. **Skip the Hideout icon** — not needed now
5. Head to the back of the zone and find the wall entrance to **The Marketplace**

---

## Zone 6 — The Marketplace

Follow the zone until you find an alleyway with large statues — proceed through to the 🔵 **Waypoint**.

Near the Waypoint, enter **The Catacombs** for the Trial of Ascendancy.

⚠️ **Ignore the Ornate Chest** (Decantur Spiritus / A Swig of Hope quest) — not worth your time.

After The Catacombs, head north, then hug the top wall going west to **The Battlefront**.

---

## Zone 7 — The Catacombs

🏆 **Trial of Ascendancy** — complete it here. Grab the Recipe Altar. Return via Waypoint.

---

## Zone 8 — The Battlefront

Acts as a crossroads. Three objectives:

1. **Head North** → find the 🔵 **Waypoint**. Near it (usually Southwest), find the **Blackguard Chest** — grab the 📦 **Ribbon Spool**
2. **Head West then North** → **The Docks**
3. **After The Docks** → head **Northeast** to **The Solaris Temple**

---

## Zone 9 — The Docks

Ignore Fairgraves and the "A Swig of Hope" quest. You're looking for a **Supply Container** on a dock that holds 📦 **Thaumetic Sulphite**.

Return to **The Battlefront** and head Northeast to **The Solaris Temple**.

---

## Zone 10 — The Solaris Temple (Level 1)

Straight walkthrough — 🔵 **Waypoint** in the middle. Proceed to Level 2.

---

## Zone 11 — The Solaris Temple (Level 2)

Follow the carpet path. At the end you'll find **Lady Dialla** near the Waypoint.

⚠️ You need both the **Ribbon Spool** and **Thaumetic Sulphite** to receive the **Infernal Talc** from Dialla. If you're missing either, go back now.

🎁 *Quest Reward: Rare Amulet*

After talking to Dialla, take the small door to the farthest room in the temple — grab the **Recipe Altar** in The Eternal Laboratory.

Now head back to **The Sewers** Waypoint, burn away the Undying Blockage, and proceed to **The Ebony Barracks**.

---

## Zone 12 — The Ebony Barracks

🔵 **Waypoint** at the start (north side). Head north.

⚔️ **GENERAL GRAVICIUS** *(Sever the Right Hand quest)*

🎁 *Quest Reward: Level 28 Gems*

This zone is another crossroads:
1. **Head Northwest** → **The Lunaris Temple** *(do this first)*
2. **Return later and head East** from Waypoint → **The Imperial Gardens**

---

## Zone 13 — The Lunaris Temple (Level 1)

Walkthrough zone — follow the carpets to the Waypoint and staircase to Level 2.

---

## Zone 14 — The Lunaris Temple (Level 2)

Long and tedious. Key landmarks:

1. Find a **long bridge ending in a staircase** — follow more of these upward
2. When you see **CARTS**: the side with **1 cart** is correct. The side with **2 carts** is wrong.
3. After the carts, use your blink skill to navigate the cage labyrinth
4. Find the large golden gate-door, pass through, and climb the final staircase to the **Portal to Piety**

⚔️ **PIETY** *(Piety's Pets quest)*

After killing her you receive the 📦 **Tower Key**. Return to town for your Skill Book from Grigor.

🎁 *Quest Reward: Passive Skill Point*

Return to **The Ebony Barracks** and head **East** of the Waypoint to **The Imperial Gardens**.

---

## Zone 15 — The Imperial Gardens

Large hub zone. Follow the stone trail to the 🔵 **Waypoint**, then complete in this order:

1. **Northwest** → **The Library** *(unlocks nearly all skill gems)*
2. **Northeast** → 🏆 **Trial of Ascendancy** *(your last one — then go Ascend!)*
3. **East** → **The Sceptre of God** *(after Ascending)*

---

## Zone 16 — The Library

Find the NPC **Siosa** and the 🔵 **Waypoint**. Talk to Siosa to begin the Archives quest. Search the Library for a **Loose Candle** to open **The Archives** zone.

---

## Zone 17 — The Archives

Collect **4 Golden Pages** from 4 Book Stands (marked with ❗ on minimap). Return to Siosa.

🎁 *Quest Reward: Level 31 Gems — a massive unlock*

Return to **The Imperial Gardens** and head to the 🏆 **Trial of Ascendancy** in the northeast.

---

## Zone 18 — The Aspirant's Plaza *(First Labyrinth)*

🏆 **Complete your first Labyrinth and Ascend!**

Tips for Lab first-timers:
1. Dying means starting over — come back stronger if needed
2. When lost: **go UP and RIGHT**
3. Save your movement skill for traps — walk carefully otherwise
4. Bring a skill gem to transfigure at the end

After Ascending, return to **The Imperial Gardens** and head **East** to **The Sceptre of God**.

---

## Zone 19 — The Sceptre of God

Three-tiered walkthrough — find and take the stairs upward at each level to reach **The Upper Sceptre of God**.

---

## Zone 20 — The Upper Sceptre of God

Three more tiers upward. The final wall door leads to **The Tower Rooftop**.

⚔️ **DOMINUS** *(Act 3 Final Boss)*

Boss tips:
- When he yells **"A Touch of God"** and raises a thunderfist — **get back immediately**
- After his first phase, he spawns a blood pool in the **Southeast corner** of the arena
- In phase two, he periodically shields himself — **stand next to him** during this to avoid Corrupted Blood stacks

After defeating Dominus, speak to Lady Dialla. The exit to Act 4 (**The Aqueduct**) opens on the right side of the arena.

---
---

# ACT 4

---

## Zone 1 — The Aqueduct

Two long parallel bridges running end to end. When one side closes, cross to the other and continue. It's very long — **don't die**, as death sends you back to the beginning.

Find the entrance to **Highgate** at the far left end of the bridges.

---

## Zone 2 — Highgate *(Act 4 Town)*

Your first destination from town is **The Dried Lake** (Southwest corner).

Vendors:
- **Kira** — weapons and armour
- **Petarus and Vanja** — potions, jewelry, caster items + free ID
- **Tasuni** — gambling and respec

---

## Zone 3 — The Dried Lake

Head South and West to find **Voll's Camp**.

⚔️ **VOLL**

🎁 *Quest Reward: Level 34 Gems*

Grab 📦 **Deshret's Banner** he drops. Return to town and take the Banner to the north of town — click **Deshret's Seal** to open the path to **The Mines**.

---

## Zone 4 — The Mines (Level 1)

Walkthrough zone. There's a **Recipe Altar** along the main path — grab it. Ignore Sulphite Mines and Niko (league content). Proceed to Level 2.

---

## Zone 5 — The Mines (Level 2)

Follow the cart tracks until you see ❗ on the minimap marking **Deshret's Spirit**, guarded by **Hammerstorm**.

Click the Spirit to complete the quest.

🎁 *Quest Reward: Passive Skill Point*

Continue through the zone to **The Crystal Veins**.

---

## Zone 6 — The Crystal Veins

Long walkthrough zone — ignore distractions. Follow the path to the 🔵 **Waypoint** and **Lady Dialla**. Talk to her, then complete both adjacent zones in either order:
- **Kaom's Dream** → Kaom's Stronghold
- **Daresso's Dream** → The Grand Arena

---

## Zone 7 — Kaom's Dream

Navigate by going **all the way RIGHT** from the start. Then go up the stairs and **all the way LEFT**. Head north to the zone entrance at the northernmost point: **Kaom's Stronghold**.

---

## Zone 8 — Kaom's Stronghold

🔵 **Waypoint** and Crafting Altar at the start — grab both. Hug the left wall north to the boss arena.

⚔️ **KAOM**

> 💡 Easy fight — dodge the telegraphed moves and that's it.

Grab 📦 **The Eye of Fury** and return to **The Crystal Veins** for **Daresso's Dream**.

---

## Zone 9 — Daresso's Dream

Narrow alleyways — follow the main path. Some rooms have gates that trap you inside until you defeat all enemies. Eventually you'll enter an arena.

⚔️ **BARKHUL** — Defeat him and proceed to **The Grand Arena**.

---

## Zone 10 — The Grand Arena

🔵 **Waypoint** and Crafting Altar at the start. Alternate between Passageways and Arenas — the exit is always at the **north end**. Continue until you reach **The Ring of Blades**.

⚔️ **DARESSO** *(4 phases — hardest is the last)*

Grab 📦 **The Eye of Desire** and return to Lady Dialla in **The Crystal Veins** to open **The Belly of the Beast**.

---

## Zone 11 — The Belly of the Beast (Level 1)

⚠️ **Stock up before starting this chain** — a long series of connected zones begins here.

Walkthrough zone. Head north-center to find the wall entrance to Level 2.

---

## Zone 12 — The Belly of the Beast (Level 2)

Head north and left to **The Bowels of the Beast**.

⚔️ **PIETY**

> 💡 Dodge her rotating beam — it moves at varying speeds. Constantly circle around her to stay clear of it.

After defeating her, talk to her, grab the Recipe Altar, and proceed up to **The Harvest**.

---

## Zone 13 — The Harvest

Follow the main tunnel to the 🔵 **Waypoint** and talk to Piety. Then loop the entire zone to defeat three bosses in any order:

1. ⚔️ **SHAVRONNE** → grab 📦 Malachai's Entrails
2. ⚔️ **DOEDRE** → grab 📦 Malachai's Lungs
3. ⚔️ **MALIGARO** → grab 📦 Malachai's Heart

Return all three items to Piety at the Waypoint to open **The Black Core**.

---

## Zone 14 — The Black Core

⚔️ **MALACHAI** *(Act 4 Final Boss)*

Boss tips:
1. When you see his large AoE attack — move directly **behind Malachai**
2. Avoid all ground effects he leaves behind
3. Watch out for his whack-a-mole tentacle attacks
4. In **The Black Heart** (phase 2): he phases at every 25% health lost — kill the **Hearts** that spawn in the corners of the arena

After slaying Malachai, use the red portal to Highgate. Collect gem reward from Dialla, then take the top-right wall exit to begin Act 5 via **The Ascent**.

---
---

# ACT 5

---

## Zone 1 — The Ascent

Three-section zone:

1. Head **North and slightly West** to a long bridge — cross it
2. Continue **North and slightly West** following the icy path to a Wall Face and Crafting Altar
3. Head **West** to the ❗ quest symbol — click the Resonator and enter the **Oriath Portal** to **The Slave Pens**

---

## Zone 2 — The Slave Pens

Large but deterministic path:
- Stay **FAR RIGHT** and go down to the **bottom-right corner**
- Then go **Southwest** until you find **Overseer Krow**

⚔️ **OVERSEER KROW**

Take the ladder to **The Overseer's Tower**.

---

## Zone 3 — The Overseer's Tower *(Act 5 Town)*

Vendors:
- **Utula** — weapons and armour
- **Lani** — potions, jewelry, caster items + free ID
- **Vilenta** — gambling and respec

Exit **Northwest** to **The Control Blocks**.

---

## Zone 4 — The Control Blocks

> 💡 **Blink shortcut:** Wherever fences split apart, you can use a blink skill to cut directly through — saves a lot of navigation time.

Midway through, spot the ❗ symbol near **Experimental Supplies** — blink through and grab the 📦 **Miasmeter**.

🎁 *Quest Reward: Passive Skill Point*

Continue to **Justicar Casticus**.

⚔️ **JUSTICAR CASTICUS** — grab 📦 **Eyes of Zeal**. Proceed to **Oriath Square**.

---

## Zone 5 — Oriath Square

🔵 **Waypoint** at the start — you'll return here multiple times. For now, follow the path past the square, cross the bridge to the east, and search the connected area for **The Templar Courts**.

---

## Zone 6 — The Templar Courts

Large walkthrough zone. After the 🔵 **Waypoint**: head **East** to the wall, then **South** to the bottom-right corner, then **West** to the next corner, then **North** to **The Chamber of Innocence**.

---

## Zone 7 — The Chamber of Innocence

Large counter-clockwise loop around the zone. Enter **The Sanctum of Innocence**.

⚔️ **INNOCENCE**

Boss tips:
- **"I am the end"** — large AoE explosion outward — **run away**
- **"I"** — a beam that follows you — dodge it by **circling around Innocence**

After defeating Innocence, speak to Sin. Exit the **RIGHT SIDE** back through the chamber, pass the Waypoint, and speak to **Bannon**. Head into **The Torched Courts**.

---

## Zone 8 — The Torched Courts

You're now running this zone in reverse (counter-clockwise). Navigate to **The Ruined Square**.

---

## Zone 9 — The Ruined Square

Four objectives from this hub:

1. **North** → find the 🔵 **Waypoint** and enter **The Ossuary**
2. **Find The Reliquary** on the opposite end of the zone from the Waypoint
3. **Kill Utula** in a side area near the center
4. **Cathedral Rooftop** → often in the opposite corner from the Waypoint side

---

## Zone 10 — The Ossuary

Clockwise loop around the zone (starting roughly at 11 o'clock). Find the 📦 **Sign of Purity** from the Tomb of the First Templar. Grab the Crafting Altar. Return to The Ruined Square.

---

## Zone 11 — The Reliquary

❗ **Quest: Kitava's Torments**

⚔️ **KITAVA, THE INSATIABLE** *(Act 5 Final Boss)*

> 💡 If you die, you spawn outside the entrance and can re-enter to resume immediately. There's no penalty for dying here — just keep going.

Familiar town — same layout as Act 1. New addition: **Faustus**, who will eventually offer Currency Exchange trading.

> 💡 After the first quest here, **Lilly Roth** will offer you **all regular gems** — a major unlock.

---

## Zone 2 — The Twilight Strand

❗ **Quest: Fallen from Grace**

Kill every single monster in the zone — the game shows a counter when 5 or fewer remain.

🎁 *Quest Reward: Passive Respec Points + All Skill Gems*

Return to town, talk to Lilly in the bottom-right corner. Then head north to **The Coast**.

---

## Zone 3 — The Coast

Same layout as Act 1. Head to the **Northeast corner** for the 🔵 **Waypoint**. Skip Tidal Island entirely this time — go straight to **The Mud Flats**.

---

## Zone 4 — The Mud Flats

Head **East** from the start until you find a broken-down shack against the north wall at the end of the zone.

⚔️ **THE DISHONORED QUEEN** — grab 📦 **Eye of Conquest**.

Head **Northwest** to find **The Karui Fortress**.

---

## Zone 5 — The Karui Fortress

Large circular zone surrounding the Fortress. Find the **interior door** entrance — it's somewhere along the inner side of the circle.

⚔️ **TUKOHAMA** *(The Father of War quest)*

Boss tips:
- Dodge his big telegraphed slam
- At 50% health, destroy the **3 totems** he spawns to resume damaging him
- After the phase, avoid his circling beam

🎁 *Quest Reward: Passive Skill Point*

Speak to Sin after the fight, then exit the Keep northeast to **The Ridge**.

---

## Zone 6 — The Ridge

Walkthrough zone — follow the linear path to **The Lower Prison**.

---

## Zone 7 — The Lower Prison

Grab the 🔵 **Waypoint**. 🏆 **Trial of Ascendancy** is usually in the **southernmost** part of the zone. After completing it, head to the opposite corner (same side) for the entrance to **Shavronne's Tower**.

---

## Zone 8 — Shavronne's Tower

4-floor tower:
- **Floor 1:** Stairs usually near the entrance
- **Floors 2–3:** Large semi-circles in opposite directions (one clockwise, one counter-clockwise)
- **Floor 4:** Central staircase to **The Prison Rooftop**

⚔️ **BRUTUS + SHAVRONNE**

> 💡 Both have telegraphed 1-shot moves. Shavronne has a mid-fight phase — dodge her lightning abilities during it.

After defeating Brutus, speak to Sin. Head left to the downstairs entrance to **The Warden's Chambers** (a short connecting area with a Crafting Altar) → then to **The Prisoner's Gate**.

---

## Zone 9 — The Prisoner's Gate

Grab the 🔵 **Waypoint**. Three objectives:

1. Find **Nessa** in the Eastern corner — just talk to her
2. Head to **The Valley of the Fire Drinker** on the opposite northern side
3. After the Valley, return here and head the opposite direction to **The Western Forest**

---

## Zone 10 — The Valley of the Fire Drinker

2-zone boss fight.

⚔️ **ABBERATH** *(two arenas)*

> 💡 Dodge telegraphed 1-shots and avoid ground effects. He phases into the second arena midway through.

🎁 *Quest Reward: Passive Skill Point*

Speak to Sin for the Pantheon. Return to **The Prisoner's Gate** and head to **The Western Forest**.

---

## Zone 11 — The Western Forest

Walkthrough zone following the path to **The Riverways**. Find the **Crafting Altar** in what was formerly Alira's Camp — follow the torches off the main path to locate it.

---

## Zone 12 — The Riverways

Two objectives:

1. Grab the 🔵 **Waypoint**, then take the side path (marked by two blocks with rods) to **The Wetlands**
2. After The Wetlands, return to the Waypoint and head **North** along the main path, then **East** to **The Southern Forest**

---

## Zone 13 — The Wetlands

Walkthrough zone to **The Spawning Ground** (usually on the north wall).

⚔️ **RYSLATHA**
- Slay her **3 eggs** (Left, Center, Right)
- She respawns in the upper center of the arena — defeat her there
- Watch out for Chaos damage in this fight

🎁 *Quest Reward: Passive Skill Point*

Return to **The Riverways** and proceed to **The Southern Forest**.

---

## Zone 14 — The Southern Forest

Large, island-heavy zone — navigate using **narrow wood bridges** between islands. Eventually reach the **Eastern corner** where the 🔵 **Waypoint** and entrance to **The Cavern of Anger** await.

---

## Zone 15 — The Cavern of Anger

Grab the 📦 **Black Flag** quest item in the middle passage. Navigate to the far side for the wall entrance to **The Beacon**.

---

## Zone 16 — The Beacon

Very large zone — find the **ocean shoreline** and follow it as it wraps up the map. Cross the rocky bridge and find two **circular platforms with columns** — the objective.

For each platform: stand inside the circle until the column retracts fully. Activate both, then find the final ❗ symbol to **light the Beacon** and click it.

A ship appears in the valley below — head there and talk to **Weylam Roth** to sail to **The Brine King's Reef**.

---

## Zone 17 — The Brine King's Reef

Large zone with no clear landmarks — stay on the **border/wall** and search for the entrance to **The Brine King's Throne**.

⚔️ **THE BRINE KING** *(Act 6 Final Boss)*

Boss tips:
- Stay **behind him**
- Don't stand too close
- During phases: **don't enter the ocean walls** and **dodge small white whirlpools** on the ground (hard to see!)
- Like all PoE1 bosses — dying just sends you back to the zone entrance, resume anytime

After victory, speak to Sin for the **Soul of the Brine King** Pantheon. Speak to **Weylam Roth** to travel to **The Bridge Encampment**.

## Zone 12 — The Dread Thicket

⚔️ **GRUTHKUL** *(Pantheon + Skill Book)*

🎁 *Quest Reward: Passive Skill Point*

## Zone 13 — The Causeway

Head **North and slightly West** to the 🔵 **Waypoint** with a Crafting Altar. Just before the zone exit, find 📦 **Kishara's Star** in Kishara's Lockbox.

🎁 *Quest Reward: Passive Skill Point*

Head to **The Vaal City**.

## Zone 14 — The Vaal City

## Zone 15 — The Temple of Decay (Level 1)

Multiple floors — at each floor, find the next path down in the **opposite corner** from where you entered.

## Zone 16 — The Temple of Decay (Level 2)

⚔️ **ARAKAALI** *(Act 7 Final Boss)*

Boss tips:
- She phases into zone corners — dodge AoEs in each phase
- Avoid the matrix-style **purple beams** crossing the arena
- At 50% health she eats Silk and enters a new phase — **dodge the purple flying discs** (very dangerous!)

After defeating her, talk to Sin and Yeena, then head north to begin **Act 8**.

Walkthrough zone — choose either direction and follow the path to **Doedre's Cesspool**.

## Zone 4 — Doedre's Cesspool

Follow the conduits to a gate. Open it, descend to **The Cauldron**. Click the Valve to spawn the boss.

- **Northeast** → **The Quay** *(do this first)*

## Zone 5 — The Quay

Challenging zone to navigate. Complete in this order:

1. Find the **Sealed Casket** *(usually early in the zone, across a short narrow bridge)* — grab 📦 **Ankh of Eternity** ⚠️ *Must be done before step 2*
2. Find the **Resurrection Site** (wall entrance) — talk to Clarissa and defeat the army
   - 🎁 *Quest Reward: Passive Skill Point*
3. Find the entrance to **The Grain Gate** *(often in the opposite direction from the Resurrection Site at a fork)*

> 💡 If you find The Grain Gate first, grab the Waypoint inside then come back.

## Zone 6 — The Grain Gate

🔵 **Waypoint** at the start. Alternate between indoor rooms and outdoor courtyards. In the **2nd outdoor courtyard**, head slightly Northwest for the **Gemling Legionnaires** encounter.

⚔️ **Defeat the Gemling Legionnaires**

🎁 *Quest Reward: Passive Skill Point*

## Zone 7 — The Imperial Fields

Walkthrough zone with 🔵 **Waypoint** in the middle. Follow the main path to the dead end, then continue in the same direction to find **The Solaris Temple** (look for courtyard flooring with statues).

---

## Zone 8 — The Solaris Temple (Level 1)

⚠️ **Ignore The Solaris Concourse** — it's a large distraction with nothing required.

## Zone 9 — The Solaris Temple (Level 2)

Follow the path to a portal at the end leading to the boss arena.

⚔️ **DAWN** *(4 telegraphed moves — easy to dodge)*

Grab 📦 **Sun Orb** after the fight. Return to **Doedre's Cesspool** (labeled Toxic Conduits on map) and take the other direction to **The Grand Promenade**.

---

## Zone 10 — The Grand Promenade

Large semi-circle — follow it counter-clockwise from the single exit to reach **The Bath House**.

---

## Zone 11 — The Bath House

Four objectives:

1. *(Optional)* Head **North** — kill **Hector Titucius** and grab 📦 **Wings of Vastiri** for a jewel reward
   - 🎁 *Quest Reward: Basic Jewel Selection*
2. Complete the 🏆 **Trial of Ascendancy** *(pull all levers in the area to unlock the trial plaque)*
3. Head **Southwest** (but still north of the Waypoint room) to **The High Gardens** — fight Yugul

---

## Zone 12 — The High Gardens

Large semi-circle path. Monsters here are very potent — dying resets the entire path. Move **quickly and carefully** to the opposite end and enter **The Pools of Terror**.

⚔️ **YUGUL** *(one of the harder mini-bosses)*

> 💡 Watch for strong attacks and cold environmental hazards. Yugul phases twice — defeat his "reflections" during each phase.

🎁 *Quest Reward: Passive Skill Point*

Return to **The Bath House** and take **The Lunaris Concourse**.

---

## Zone 13 — The Lunaris Concourse

🔵 **Waypoint** slightly north of center. Head **North and slightly East** to **The Lunaris Temple**. After the temple, return to the Waypoint and go **East** to **The Harbour Bridge**.

---

## Zone 14 — The Lunaris Temple (Level 1)

Follow the blue carpet. At the large cathedral-like room, head **north and east** through the forking rooms to find the Waypoint and entrance to Level 2.

---

## Zone 15 — The Lunaris Temple (Level 2)

This zone is notoriously difficult to navigate. No clear landmarks, many dead ends, cage labyrinth sections.

> 💡 Sorry — just push through it. Use your blink skill liberally in the cage sections.

Find the portal to the boss arena.

⚔️ **DUSK** *(easy fight — similar to Dawn)*

Grab 📦 **Moon Orb**. Return to **The Lunaris Concourse** and head **East and South** from the Waypoint to **The Harbour Bridge**.

## Zone 16 — The Harbour Bridge

⚔️ **SOLARIS + LUNARIS** *(Act 8 Final Boss)*

> 💡 They alternate between being targetable — focus only on the one currently active.

Large zone — three objectives:

Large zone — ignore the two side distractions. Head to **The Sand Pit**.

> 💡 At ~60% health she phases and burrows underground. Follow her burrowing path **slowly and incrementally** — running ahead too fast prevents her from following. Reach the end for the final arena.

> 💡 Optional: kill **Boulderback** mini-boss for 📦 **Calendar of Fortune** *(Passive Respec Point)*

## Zone 7 — The Boiling Lake

⚔️ **THE BASILISK** *(very easy — stand behind him)*

🏆 **Trial of Ascendancy** — usually near the entrance. Follow the straightforward path to the 🔵 **Waypoint**, then continue to **The Quarry**.
